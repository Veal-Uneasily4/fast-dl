Kart Wheelchair

by drzj 

https://gamebanana.com/members/2161064

This is a fun map based on a Race Circuit where the vehicles are wheelchairs.

Features:
- 24 slots (wheelchairs) for competitors (TTs) and 8 for spectators (CTs).
- A Camera Area where you can see different parts of the circuit.
- A Pit Lane for wheelchairs that will not be used.
- A total of 17 Curves on the circuit.
- A Countdown Button at the start.

Enjoy it!
